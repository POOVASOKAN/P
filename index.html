Case Studies

  • Ensuite Bathroom • £8,000-£10,000

    Project duration: 2.5 Weeks

    Project description: With the client being 80+ years old, we prioritized practicality and safety. The project involved redesigning and fitting a new bathroom, including moving and removing pipework, 1st fixing electrics, tiling, and fitting the suite. The client was delighted with the results.

  • Luxury Bathroom • £10,000-£15,000

    Project duration: 2.5 Weeks

    Project description: For a client developing seven properties, we completed the main bathroom with a freestanding bath, basins, towel radiators, and showers. The project included hot and cold pipework, waste pipes, plastering, tiling, and fitting the bathroom suite to the highest standard.

    Project duration: ½ Days

    Project description: After a site visit, we replaced the client's old boiler with a new Baxi MAIN eco compact 30kW boiler.

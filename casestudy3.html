Case Study - Combi boiler Installation

Our client required a cost-effective boiler replacement, and we installed a new Baxi MAIN eco compact 30kW boiler. The project involved draining the system, repiping, and installing a wireless thermostat for convenient heating control, ensuring safety and efficiency throughout

Project description

Our client contacted us after their boiler had stopped working. After a thorough site visit and consultation, we determined that replacing the old boiler with a new Baxi MAIN eco compact 30kW boiler would be the most cost-effective solution. The project involved draining the existing system, removing outdated pipework and electrics, and installing the new boiler in the appropriate location. We then repiped the new boiler to connect with the existing system and ensured everything was securely in place. The flue was carefully measured, cut, and installed to fit perfectly, and the safety blow-off device was routed safely outside the property. We also completed the necessary electrical work, including the installation of a wireless thermostat for easy temperature control. Once satisfied with the setup, we commissioned the boiler following the manufacturer’s instructions, completed the warranty registration, and notified the building authority about the new installation, ensuring a safe and compliant setup

How we did it ?

We expertly replaced the old boiler with a new Baxi MAIN eco compact 30kW, ensuring precise repiping and secure flue installation. The project also included integrating a wireless thermostat for easy control. Our meticulous approach delivered a safe, efficient, and reliable heating solution.

Case Study - Ensuite Bathroom Makeover

The client, an 80+ year old individual, required a bathroom renovation that prioritized safety and practicality. The existing space needed to be optimized to ensure ease of use while maintaining a modern aesthetic.

Project description

With the client on this example, we needed to really use the space wisely as we had to make sure it was practical since the client was 80+ years old, so it had to be safe and practical. Firstly, we had to identify the system being used for the bathroom to ensure isolating what was needed. After doing this, it was safe to work on the bathroom! We started by ripping out the old bathroom. Then, we redesigned the bathroom, moved pipework, removed old pipework that was no longer needed, and moved the necessary components to their new locations. After we did this and the fittings were secured and tested, it was time to first fix the electrics needed for the bath, radiator, shower point, and lights, including both main lights and a sensor light for the toilet area. The room was then plasterboarded and plastered. Once dried, we tiled the entire bathroom to ensure a flawless, maintenance-free finish. Finally, we installed the suite, starting with the shower, then moving onto the toilet, basin, bath, and radiator. Our client was over the moon with the results and found it very practical to use!

How we did it ?

We meticulously redesigned the bathroom, optimizing the space for safety and practicality. Our team ensured flawless execution, from rerouting plumbing to installing modern fixtures, resulting in a highly functional and aesthetic space.

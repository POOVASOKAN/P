Case Study - Luxury Bathroom Makeover

Our client was developing seven luxury properties, each featuring multiple high-end bathrooms. We meticulously planned and executed every step, from the initial pipework to the final fittings, ensuring flawless results throughout

Project description

For this project, our client was developing seven luxury properties, each valued at over £1 million and featuring 3-4 bathrooms. The main bathrooms were designed to include a freestanding bath, dual basins, two towel radiators, and dual showers. We began by first fixing the bathrooms, starting with the hot and cold pipework to each designated area. All fittings were securely installed within the walls, with pipework strategically placed to connect with the necessary fixtures. After completing the pipework, we installed the waste pipes, directing them out of the property and into the main 4-inch soil pipe leading to the main drainage system. Once this was done, the rooms were plasterboarded and skimmed, ready for tiling. After tiling the appropriate areas, we installed the bathroom suites, ensuring everything functioned perfectly. With careful planning and precise execution, we delivered flawless results that met our client's high standards.

How we did it ?

We meticulously redesigned the bathroom, optimizing the space for safety and practicality. Our team ensured flawless execution, from rerouting plumbing to installing modern fixtures, resulting in a highly functional and aesthetic space.
